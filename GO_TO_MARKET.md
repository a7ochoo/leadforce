# LeadForce - Go-to-Market Strategy

## 🎯 Market Opportunity

**TAM (Total Addressable Market):**
- 150,000 real estate agents in France
- Average lead qualification time: 3-4 hours/day
- Average agent salary: €2,500/month
- Cost of wasted time: €300-400/month per agent
- **Total TAM: €45-60M annually in France alone**

**SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market):**
- 50,000 solo agents + small agencies (not mega-franchises)
- Willing to adopt SaaS tools
- Digital-savvy
- **SAM: €15-25M annually**

**SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market - Year 1):**
- 1,000 paying users (conservative)
- 49€ Agent plan
- 199€ Agency plan
- €500K-€1M ARR

## 📊 Customer Segments

### Segment 1: Solo Agents (80% of market)
- Profile: Independent agent, 5-20 deals/year
- Pain: Manual lead qualification (3h/day wasted)
- Willingness to pay: 49€/month (0.5h saved = ROI)
- Decision maker: Themselves
- **TAM: 120,000 agents**

### Segment 2: Small Agencies (15% of market)
- Profile: 3-10 agents, owner-managed
- Pain: Team efficiency, consistent follow-up
- Willingness to pay: 199€/month (5 agents)
- Decision maker: Agency owner
- **TAM: 25,000 agencies**

### Segment 3: Large Agencies (5% of market)
- Profile: 50+ agents, enterprise tools
- Pain: Lead routing, team management
- Willingness to pay: 999€+/month (custom)
- Decision maker: Operations manager
- **TAM: 5,000 agencies - NOT FOCUS YEAR 1**

## 🎬 Launch Strategy

**Goal:** Validate product-market fit with early adopters

- Deploy MVP on Vercel
- Recruit 50 beta testers from personal network
- Free access for 3 months
- Weekly check-ins for feedback
- Collect testimonials & case studies
- Metrics:
  - 50 active beta users
  - 90%+ daily active rate
  - NPS > 40
  - 3+ feature requests per user

**Activities:**
- Personal outreach to 50 agents
- WhatsApp group for beta feedback
- Weekly product updates
- Gather video testimonials

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Month 4)

- Metrics:

## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### Tier 1: Agent Solo
- Price: **49€/month**
- Target: 70% of market
- Features:
  - Unlimited leads
  - 4 channels (Gmail + WhatsApp)
  - Scoring & reports
  - Mobile app (basic)
- Messaging: "€50 = 3 hours saved = 1 deal closer"

### Tier 2: Agency
- Price: **199€/month** (5 agents included)
- Target: 25% of market
- Extra agents: **20€/month each**
- Features:
  - Unlimited agents
  - Team dashboard
  - Lead assignment
  - Advanced reporting
- Messaging: "€200 = entire team efficiency"

### Tier 3: Enterprise (Future)
- Price: **Custom**
- Target: Large agencies (Year 2+)
- Features: Custom integration, dedicated support

### Trial
- Duration: **7 days** (no credit card)
- Conversion target: 15%
- Messaging: "Try free, no commitment"
