# LeadForce - Pitch Deck

## 🎯 The Problem

❌ Real estate agents spend **3-4 hours per day** manually qualifying leads
❌ Miss follow-up opportunities due to volume
❌ No standardized scoring system
❌ Waste time on low-quality prospects

**Cost: 20-30 hours/week = 1 lost potential deal/week**

## 💡 The Solution

✅ **LeadForce**: Automatic lead qualification using AI
- Receives leads from WhatsApp, Gmail, announcements
- Claude AI classifies & scores in real-time
- Agent gets notification + daily report
- **Saves 3-4 hours/day** per agent

## 📊 How It Works

```
Prospect sends message
        ↓
LeadForce receives (WhatsApp/Gmail/etc)
        ↓
Claude AI classifies (Respondent/Searcher)
        ↓
AI asks qualifying questions
        ↓
Prospect submits form
        ↓
AI generates score (BON/MOYEN/FAIBLE)
        ↓
Agent gets instant notification
        ↓
Daily report at 6pm
```
