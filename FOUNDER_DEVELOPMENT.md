## 🧠 Mindset Development

### Challenge #1: Imposter Syndrome
**Issue:** Ahmed feels like he "got lucky" with MVP

**Solution:**
- Document what you've built (60+ files, 12K+ LOC)
- Remind yourself: You built this alone in 7 days
- Successful people feel this way
- Action: Write weekly wins journal
- Mentor: Find a successful founder buddy

### Challenge #2: Perfectionism
**Issue:** Always wanting to improve instead of ship

**Solution:**
- "Perfect is the enemy of good"
- Metrics > feelings
- Ship → Measure → Iterate
- Action: Set shipping deadlines
- Mantra: "Done > Perfect"

### Challenge #3: Burnout Risk
**Issue:** Working 80+ hours/week (unhealthy)

**Solution:**
- Set work hours (9am-6pm, no weekends)
- Schedule 2 days off/week
- Exercise 3x/week
- Sleep 8 hours
- Action: Time blocking in calendar
- Mantra: "Sustainable > Sustainable"

### Challenge #4: Loneliness (Solo Founder)
**Issue:** No co-founder to bounce ideas off

**Solution:**
- Find founder community (F6S, Nomadlist)
- Weekly mentor calls (2x)
- Founder dinners (1x/month)
- Co-working spaces
- Action: Join founder group
- Network: Build advisory board
